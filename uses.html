Uses of Horticultural Crops

Fruits

Fruits are rich in vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants.

  • Apples: Boost immunity and improve digestion.

  • Bananas: Provide instant energy and aid in muscle recovery.

  • Oranges: High in Vitamin C and support skin health.

  • Grapes: Contain resveratrol, good for heart health.

  • Papayas: Improve digestion and skin health.

  • Mangoes: Boost eyesight and immunity.

Vegetables

Vegetables are a vital source of nutrients and fiber.

  • Carrots: Improve vision and skin health.

  • Spinach: Rich in iron and supports bone health.

  • Tomatoes: Contain lycopene, good for the heart.

  • Broccoli: Packed with antioxidants and fiber.

  • Potatoes: Great source of carbohydrates and potassium.

  • Cauliflower: Supports digestion and detoxification.

Flowers

Flowers are used for decoration, perfumes, and medicinal purposes.

  • Roses: Used in perfumes and skincare products.

  • Marigolds: Used in religious ceremonies and pest control.

  • Lavender: Known for its calming properties.

  • Chamomile: Used in teas for relaxation.

  • Hibiscus: Used in hair care and tea infusions.

  • Jasmine: Aromatherapy and skincare benefits.

Herbs

Herbs are used for flavoring food, medicine, and aromatherapy.

  • Basil: Antibacterial, boosts immunity.

  • Mint: Freshens breath and aids digestion.

  • Thyme: Fights bacteria, used in cooking.

  • Coriander: Improves cholesterol levels.

  • Oregano: Antioxidant-rich and antimicrobial.

  • Lemongrass: Reduces anxiety and bloating.

Spices

Spices enhance taste and offer medicinal benefits.

  • Turmeric: Anti-inflammatory and healing.

  • Cinnamon: Balances blood sugar levels.

  • Black Pepper: Improves digestion.

  • Cardamom: Refreshes breath and aids digestion.

  • Clove: Relieves toothaches and improves immunity.

  • Cumin: Good for digestion and detox.

Nuts

Nuts are nutrient-dense and support brain and heart health.

  • Almonds: Improve brain function and skin health.

  • Cashews: Support bone health and energy levels.

  • Walnuts: Rich in omega-3s for heart health.

  • Pistachios: Aid weight management and eye health.

  • Hazelnuts: Reduce inflammation and support the heart.

  • Brazil Nuts: Excellent source of selenium for thyroid function.
