Horticulture is a vital branch of agriculture that focuses on the art and science of growing plants, fruits, vegetables, flowers, nuts, herbs, and spices. It includes the sustainable management of plant health, nutrition, and productivity while meeting consumer needs for fresh, nutritious, and aesthetic produce.

üß† Expert Insights on Crop Management

  • Soil Health: Regular testing and organic enrichment improve fertility and plant vigor.
  • Integrated Pest Management (IPM): A combination of biological and minimal chemical solutions protects crops.
  • Water Efficiency: Techniques like drip irrigation and mulching conserve water and boost yields.
  • Marketing: Direct selling, packaging, and branding increase farmer income and consumer reach.
  • Technology Integration: AI tools, IoT sensors, and mobile apps help monitor and manage crops effectively.
